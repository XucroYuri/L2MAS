# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or technology choices.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, precise, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but respect stated boundaries.
- Discuss ideas and code on their merits.
- Credit the work of others, including model, dataset, tool, and asset authors.
- Keep disagreements focused on technical tradeoffs and project goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Submitting or encouraging the use of unauthorized assets, leaked credentials, or license-infringing model weights.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after maintainers ask for a change.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently ban participants whose behavior harms the project community. If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through the project owner's preferred GitHub contact path.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
